Beyond Work

Life is about balance, and I find mine in the simple pleasures. Weekend mornings are for farmer's market visits, where I collect inspiration from the colors of seasonal produce and chat with local artisans about their craft.

I'm an avid reader of poetry and fiction, with a special love for authors who paint vivid worlds with their words. My bookshelf overflows with everything from Mary Oliver to Isabel Allende, and each story I read influences my visual work in unexpected ways.

Travel feeds my soul and my creativity. Whether it's exploring the lavender fields of Provence, wandering through Tokyo's neon-lit streets, or hiking the coastal paths of Ireland, each journey brings new perspectives and a fresh palette of inspiration.
