About the Author

Mary DW is a USA Today bestselling romance author known for her emotionally charged love stories that blend passion with heart. She writes contemporary and historical romance novels featuring strong heroines and swoon-worthy heroes.

With over a dozen published novels and a devoted fanbase, Mary creates stories that celebrate love in all its beautiful, complicated forms.
